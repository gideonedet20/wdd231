About Us

Championing business in the Garden City

For over three decades, the Chamber has supported entrepreneurs across Trans-Amadi, Diobu, and beyond — from oil and gas service providers to market traders and tech startups. We advocate for fair policy, host regular networking events, and help new businesses find their footing.
